## Common questions

**Notifications aren't appearing.**
Open Settings on your iPhone, find Asterial, and check that Allow Notifications is on. If they still don't show, send yourself a preview from inside the app: Settings tab, Notifications, Send me a preview in 5 seconds.

**I want to change my birthday or focus areas.**
Open the Settings tab inside Asterial, tap Your profile, then tap any field to edit.

**How do I delete my data?**
Settings tab, scroll to the bottom, tap Reset everything. Or just delete the app. Nothing is stored anywhere except your phone, so deleting the app deletes everything.

**The app crashed.**
Send me an email with what you were doing when it crashed and I'll fix it.
