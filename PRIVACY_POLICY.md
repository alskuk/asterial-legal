# Privacy Policy — Asterial

**Effective: [launch date — TBD]**
**Last updated: 2026-04-22**

## The short version

Asterial collects nothing. Ever.

Your answers, your custom times, and your friends abroad list all
live on your phone only. There are no accounts, no passwords, no
servers, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no third parties.
If you delete the app, everything goes with it.

---

## The longer version

### What data Asterial handles

The following information is entered by you and stored only on your
device, in iOS's local preferences store:

- Your first name
- Your birthday (used to calculate your sun sign)
- The two focus areas you pick
- Your spiritual style (Warm, Cosmic, or Both)
- Your reading style (how often you want to be notified)
- Any personal times you add (e.g. "Morning anchor at 09:00")
- Any friends abroad you add (name, city, timezone, chosen hours)

That's it.

### Where your data goes

Nowhere.

Asterial has no backend. There is no sign-in, no cloud sync, no
server we operate. We never see, read, or store any of the
information above. It does not leave your device at any point, for
any reason.

### Notifications

Asterial uses Apple's local notifications (`UNUserNotificationCenter`)
to deliver reminders at your chosen times. Local notifications are
scheduled by iOS on your device and never pass through our servers —
because we don't have any.

The copy you see in a notification is assembled on your device, at
the moment it's delivered, from content bundled inside the app.

### Analytics, tracking, and ads

Asterial has no analytics SDK. No Firebase. No Mixpanel. No
Amplitude. No Google Analytics. No Facebook SDK. No third-party
cookies. No IDFA collection. No ads, ever.

### Children

Asterial is rated 4+ but is intended for users aged 13 and older. We
do not knowingly collect any personal information from children,
because we don't collect any personal information from anyone.

### Your rights

Because we hold no data about you, there is nothing to access,
correct, export, or delete at the server level. To delete everything
Asterial knows about you:

- Open **Settings → Reset everything** inside the app, or
- Delete the app from your iPhone.

Either action erases all five onboarding answers, your custom
times, your friends abroad list, your notification preferences, and
every pending notification.

### Third parties

Asterial does not share anything with third parties, because
Asterial does not have anything to share.

The app does depend on Apple's iOS platform (for local storage and
local notifications). Apple's own privacy policies apply to the
operating system; you can read them at apple.com/privacy.

### Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at
the top and note what changed. There is no email list to notify
because there is no email list — you can check this page any time.

### Contact

If you have questions, suggestions, or want to tell us something
broke, email: `hello@asterial.app`

That inbox is read by one human. We don't keep emails longer than we
need to answer them.
